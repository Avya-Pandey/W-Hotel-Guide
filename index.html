What to do?




WET Pool

The W hotel's pool is the largest outdoor pool in SG. And guess what? The pool is open 24/7. So even though you need to book a slot, it, you can have a dip anytime.

Tanjong Beach

It's time to build some sandcastles. On Sentosa Island, there are three beaches to visit, of which Tanjong Beach is the one closest to W Hotel. Tanjong Beach is famous for its rare white sand and pellucid waters.

HydroDash

Allow the games to begin by challenging friends, family, and loved ones around our adrenaline-pumping floating obstacle course. Our inflatable playground is a family-friendly attraction that offers activities, excitement, and delight for people of all ages.

Peak Time - 4 p.m.

Fort Siloso

A wonderful outdoor museum with many well-preserved relics and carefully managed exhibitions. Not a history fan ? Then explore the nearby nature trails and walks. Then perhaps have a lovely dinner somewhere here at the end of the day?




Cuisine




The Kitchen Table

The W Singapore's bright, airy and cheery all-day dining restaurant promises hearty breakfasts, delicious lunches and indulgent dinners. It is also the stage for the W's legendary buffet brunches. Even the pickiest eaters can find something amidst the live stations, variety of desserts (which includes a chocolate fountain), grills, carvery, seafood, local food and more.

Royal Taj @ Village Hotel

Royal Taj definitely exceeds expectations of many. Great food, exceptional service, and very impressive setting. If you're in Sentosa and craving Indian food, this is the place to go for!!

Coastes

If you're looking for a quiet supper with the family, Coastes is the place to go at Siloso Beach, one of Singapore's nicest beaches. The safe and clean sand and shallow waters will keep the kids occupied. The menu includes kid-friendly favorites including pasta, burgers, and sandwiches, as well as a Curry Laksa for those who prefer Asian cuisine. Coastes is usually a terrific idea for all-day meals or perhaps a beer by the beach.

FOC Sentosa Restaurant

Located at the east end of tranquil Palawan Beach, the tasteful and airy beachfront space is resplendent with bright interiors and panoramic views of the sea. The second-storey private events space can be reserved and transformed to accommodate intimate beach weddings, private parties, or corporate events.

Casserole

Casserole Singapore invites you to come by to enjoy its namesake dish - the casserole. Located at Dine on 3 in Shangri-La Rasa Sentosa, Singapore on Siloso Road, this popular restaurant attracts foodies from all over the island with their hearty, flavour-packed options. Open every day of the week for dinner, Casserole offers guests a place to sit back, relax and unwind with food from around the world. Although casseroles are the highlight here, you can also find an array of other dishes. Just ensure you bring your biggest appetite when dining here - you're going to want to eat a whole lot.
